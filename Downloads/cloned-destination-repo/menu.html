Mexican Food Menu

Item Description
Tacos Soft or crispy tortillas filled with various fillings such as beef, chicken, or vegetables, topped with salsa, cheese, and guacamole.
Enchiladas Tortillas rolled and filled with meat, cheese, or beans, covered in chili sauce and baked until deliciously melted.
Tamales Masa dough filled with meat, cheese, or vegetables, wrapped in a corn husk and steamed to perfection.
Guacamole A delicious avocado-based dip made with mashed avocados, lime juice, onions, tomatoes, and spices.
Chiles Rellenos Poblano peppers stuffed with cheese, meat, or beans, battered and fried until golden and served with tomato sauce.
Ceviche A refreshing seafood dish made with raw fish or shrimp marinated in citrus juice, mixed with onions, tomatoes, and cilantro.
Churros Delicious fried dough pastries dusted with cinnamon sugar, often served with chocolate dipping sauce.
Flan A creamy caramel custard dessert with a smooth texture and rich flavor.
